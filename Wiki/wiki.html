Understanding Financial Terms: A Beginner's Guide

1. Asset

An asset is anything of value that can be converted into cash...

2. Liability

A liability is an obligation or debt that a person or organization owes to another party...

3. Equity

Equity represents ownership interest in a company or property...

4. Interest Rate

An interest rate is the percentage of a loan or investment that is charged as interest over a specified period of time...

5. Portfolio

A portfolio is a collection of financial assets owned by an individual or institution...

6. Capital Gains

Capital gains are the profits earned from the sale of an investment or asset that has increased in value over time...

7. Dividend

A dividend is a payment made by a corporation to its shareholders...

8. Budget

A budget is a financial plan that outlines projected income and expenses over a specific period of time...

Conclusion

Understanding these basic financial terms is essential for making informed decisions about your personal or business finances...
